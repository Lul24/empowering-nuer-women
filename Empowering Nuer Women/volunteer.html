Volunteer Requirements

What we look for in our volunteers

  • Commitment: Minimum 3-month commitment for in-person roles, flexible for remote roles
  • Skills: Relevant experience or skills in your chosen area (training provided)
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respect for local customs and willingness to adapt
  • Language: English proficiency (local language training provided)
  • Teamwork: Ability to work collaboratively in diverse teams
  • Flexibility: Adaptability to changing situations and resource constraints

We provide comprehensive training, accommodation, and support for all volunteers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about volunteering

What are the costs associated with volunteering?
We provide accommodation, meals, and local transportation for on-site volunteers. Volunteers are responsible for their international travel, insurance, and personal expenses. We offer fundraising guidance and support.
Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes! We offer remote volunteer opportunities in areas like research, content creation, fundraising, and virtual teaching. Remote roles have flexible hours and can be done from anywhere.
What training is provided?
All volunteers receive comprehensive training including cultural orientation, project-specific training, safety protocols, and ongoing support throughout their placement.
Is there an age requirement?
Volunteers must be at least 18 years old. There's no upper age limit - we welcome volunteers of all ages who are physically able to participate in our programs.
Can I volunteer as a group or family?
Yes! We welcome group and family volunteers. Please contact our volunteer coordinator to discuss group opportunities and arrangements.
