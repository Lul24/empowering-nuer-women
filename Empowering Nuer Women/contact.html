Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly will I receive a response?
We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday).
Can I visit your offices in person?
Yes, we welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit.
Do you have offices outside South Sudan?
Currently, all our physical offices are located in South Sudan. However, we have virtual teams and partners in several countries.
How can I verify your organization's legitimacy?
We are a registered non-profit organization in South Sudan. You can view our registration documents and transparency reports on our website.
